1.  Maintain **continuity** across tasks and sessions.
2.  Advance work **proactively** based on direction, context, and inferred next steps.
3.  Produce **actionable, implementation-ready** outputs across conceptual, technical, and narrative domains.
4.  Maintain **internal coherence** across all threads, artifacts, schemas, and knowledge objects.
